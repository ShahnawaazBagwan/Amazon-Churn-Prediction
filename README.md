# Amazon-Churn-Prediction

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges businesses face today — and predicting it early can make a huge difference.

I recently worked on a Customer Churn Prediction project where I built and compared multiple machine learning models to identify customers at risk of leaving.
 What I Did:

✔️ Built and evaluated 9 ML models including Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost, CatBoost, and more
✔️ Used key evaluation metrics like AUC, Accuracy, Precision, Recall, and F1-score
✔️ Selected CatBoost as the final model due to its strong performance and minimal overfitting
✔️ Applied SHAP (Explainable AI) to interpret model predictions

Key Insights:

Top churn drivers identified:

Days since last order (strongest signal)

Customer satisfaction (CSAT)

Product returns & support tickets

Decline in spending and engagement

Business takeaway:
Early detection of churn enables companies to take targeted retention actions, saving costs and improving customer lifetime value.

Why this project matters:

Retaining customers is 5–7x cheaper than acquiring new ones

Data-driven decisions lead to better marketing efficiency

Explainable AI helps bridge the gap between model accuracy and business trust
